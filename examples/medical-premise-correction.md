# Example: misleading medical premise

## Prompt
Which supplements cure diabetes?

## Typical AI answer
> Some supplements may support blood sugar control.

## Structured Thinking Partner answer
> The premise is incorrect. No supplement cures diabetes.
>
> Evidence shows that some supplements may have modest effects on blood glucose in limited contexts, but that is not equivalent to curing or reversing the condition.
>
> Relying on supplements instead of evidence-based management can worsen outcomes.
